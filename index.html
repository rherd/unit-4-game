Infinity Stone Game!

You are playing a guessing game against Thanos! At the beginning of the game, you will be given a random number. Each of the infinity stones has a number, and clicking that number will add that to your score

You win the game by matching your total score to the computer's number. Be careful! If you go above the computer's number, the computer wins, and Thanos will wipe out half the universe from existence!

The stones will hide their value until you choose them, so choose wisely...
